# Code of Conduct

MetricDrive is a personal research portfolio project, but public discussion and
contributions should still be respectful, constructive, and evidence oriented.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Critique ideas, code, metrics, and claims rather than people.
- Keep discussions public-safe: do not request, disclose, or imply access to
  private company data, credentials, or internal systems.
- Be clear about uncertainty, limitations, and reproducibility.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Encouraging unsafe use of autonomous-driving code as a real driving system.
- Misrepresenting this project as affiliated with Waymo or any other company.

## Enforcement

Issues, pull requests, and discussions that violate these expectations may be
edited, closed, or removed. Contributors may be blocked from participating in
the repository.
